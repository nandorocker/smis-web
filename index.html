Hello

My name is Zuzanna Rajewska. I'm a Berlin-based freelance designer with a background in Fine Arts, passionate and inspired by minimal and modern design. I have an eye for color, styles and trends.

I've done work for some big brands like Microsoft and eBay, as well as renowned underground bands and venues. Projects include design of event materials, icons, banners, posters, album artwork and more.

Microsoft Build conference

Designs for printed and digital materials for Microsoft Build conference in Warsaw, Poland (2016). Project includes interior design elements, lanyards, posters, streeming graphics etc. I had to strictly follow brand and event guidelines.

Watches for Avon

Watches designs with leopard pattern motive. Shape of the watch case and theme was specified in the client’s brief.

Cover for Partia

Cover design for legendary Warsaw’s alternative band Partia (1993-2003). Record consists a live recordings of the last band’s concert. Image used on the cover comes from Karel Saudek - czech comic book illustrator. The goal was to design a cover similar to previous ones, but also make it look more modern.

Banners for eBay

Banners are made according to new brand guide lines (color groups, font designed specially for eBay, not posed and simple mood images, products on a plain background).

Moodboards for Oriflame

Moodboards for possible fashion trends in 2015 and examples of products (jewellery, watches, bags and accesories).
